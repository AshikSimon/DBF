Structures


What does the Structures Team do?

The team uses SolidWorks to create a CAD model of the plane; individual parts and assemblies. We perform stress and strain analysis on critical components of the aircraft. Load cases are used to identify parts of the aircraft that are susceptible to failure.

As a member of the team, you will:

  • Collaborate with all members in the process of transferring a concept into a physical design through the use of CAD.
  • Practice the process of design within the limited scope of fabrication methods.
  • Work closely with the fabrication team to determine the best method of turning your designs into a physical reality.

Structures Team members will learn:

  • Solidworks CAD design and Finite Element Analysis methods.
  • How to prepare CAD Designs for laser cutting, 3D printing, and CNC milling production.
  • Teamwork and coordination skills necessary to generate designs with other subteam agendas in mind.
