Fabrications


What does the fabrication team do?

The fabrication team is responsible for manufacturing and assembling components of the plane. We are in charge of transforming and integrating designs into a physical end product. Once completed, the plane is tested for its integrity and performance.

What will I learn in fabrication?

  • Foam Cutting
  • Vacuum Bagging
  • Electronics Installation and Wiring
  • Technical Writing
  • Mechanical Design

How do I get involved with the fabrication team?

DBF values getting hands-on experiences. You can join the fabrication team with any level of experience and academic background. If you are interested to learn more, please reach out to DBF: dbf at ucsd dot edu
