New Members

The UCSD DBF project team seeks students interested in cultivating their knowledge of the design and build techniques for model airplane flight. In doing so, participants will be part of an entire project team, learning the engineering work ethics and design processes required to see ideas through to success. This is a very hands-on project and provides a medium for students to apply their knowledge and passion towards.

Involvement

The project team is broken down into functional teams for an effective division of labor, but it also allows members to specialize in a particular discipline. Each team has a Lead that will train and instruct their members. You need not join with any prior knowledge or experience in RC aircraft design and manufacturing. We will teach you everything!

Recruitment

The recruitment cycle occurs during the first half of Fall Quarter of each academic year, and training starts during the second half. However, it's never too late to join! Our leadership will always work with you to figure out how exactly you can participate on the project that ensures you are learning and contributing in a meaningful and fulfilling way. We are always looking for interested pilots, experienced or not!
